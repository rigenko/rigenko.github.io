About company

The company was founded in 1995 by a group of like-minded enthusiasts. Over the years, the company has grown rapidly, opening offices and representative offices throughout Europe. Today, the company is actively engaged in promoting the Asian market.

Today, the company has more than 300 employees, whose efforts have implemented about 800 projects. The audience of the company's games is 50 million players per month and up to 10 million players per day. In total, the company has 200 million installations.

Social responsibility

We understand that we are also part of the community. That is why we are obliged to lend a helping hand - to hungry children, lonely elderly people, needy families, victims of natural disasters ... We take part in humanitarian and charitable actions aimed at helping the victims.

In addition, as an employer, we care about the preservation of core values, comfortable jobs, as well as sound management, which in turn helps to unlock the potential, abilities and will of each person.

Environmental responsibility - reducing the harmful effects on the environment

The natural resources of the earth are not infinite - and we pay special attention to this in every segment of our work. We know that our impact is small, but we also believe that every step counts.

We make every effort to ensure that our materials do not harm the environment. We have invested significant resources in our company's environmental projects. And we always try to be where society needs us. Finally, we try to lead a healthy lifestyle at work, at our workplaces.

Environmental responsibility in our offices

  • Waste sorting

    Waste recycling is a very important part of our daily work. Almost all office waste is recycled. In addition, we stopped using disposable tableware.

  • Rational energy consumption

    In our offices, each desktop is equipped with an energy-saving lamp with sensors that regulate the amount of light during the day.

  • Ecological gifts

    We are interested in sharing our enthusiasm, especially with our children. Our Christmas gifts are always recyclable and made from natural materials.
